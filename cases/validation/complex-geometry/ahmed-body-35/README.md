# Ahmed Body (35 deg slant)

## Physics

At 35 deg slant, the flow separates at the slant edge and forms a large recirculation bubble. This destroys the trailing vortices and reduces drag by ~20% compared to 25 deg. The transition from attached to separated slant flow occurs at ~30 deg.

## Flow Features

- Separated recirculation bubble on the slant
- No strong trailing vortices
- Cd=0.231 (significantly lower than 25 deg)
- High lift: Cl=0.165
