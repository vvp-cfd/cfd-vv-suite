# Ahmed Body (25 deg slant)

## Physics

Classic automotive aerodynamics benchmark. The Ahmed body is a simplified car shape with a slanted rear surface. At 25 deg slant, the flow remains attached on the slant with trailing vortices from side edges. This gives maximum drag.

## Geometry

Length L=1.044 m, height H=0.288 m, width W=0.389 m, ground clearance 0.05 m.
Slant angle 25 deg, urethane foam model. Mounted on struts in wind tunnel.

## Flow Features

- Attached flow on the slant
- Strong longitudinal trailing vortices from C-pillars
- Wake dominated by vortex shedding
- Cd=0.285 (highest among slant angles)
